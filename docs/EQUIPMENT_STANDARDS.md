# Equipment Standards

## VAV electrical allowance

Electrical allowance is entered as an editable cost per VAV:

`VAV quantity x electrical cost per VAV`

The value is included in estimate totals and report exports.

## Sylk sensors

Honeywell TR-series sensors are material items connected over the Sylk bus. They:

- contribute zero AI/UI physical points;
- contribute one Sylk device per selected sensor;
- remain included in material quantity and cost.
